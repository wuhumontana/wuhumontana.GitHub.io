3. [20 Points] Controls are an important part of helping mitigate risks. However, controls in place without proper monitoring may provide a false sense of security for an organization.
You've been asked been your management to address the following concerns about an implemented control related to applying security patches:

  • What are the consequences of not applying security patches on a routine basis?

High-risk vulnerabilities may lead to malicious intrusion and use of company computers by hackers, resulting in company data leakage and major property losses.
For example,computers in a TSMC production base were infected by the "Eternal Bule" virus because they did not install the security patch.This secutiy incident lead to the shutdown of many important production line, and revenue fell by 3% in the third quarter.

  • Are there any potential impacts to the business when applying these security patches?

This may cause the computer run slower, blue screen, or even terminate
For example,a security patch applied by NASA caused monitoring equipment inside a large engineering furnace to shut down, sparking a fire that destroyed spacecraft hardware inside the furnace. Computer restarts caused by the software update also prevented the alarm from firing, leaving the fire unnoticed for 3.5 hours.

(c) [20 Points] Utilizing the recent (January 2022) disclosure of a vulnerability in AWS Glue service (see link below), explain how users of AWS services are potentially at risk and the mitigations that must be put into place by Amazon's security team to ensure each user's environment remains as secure as possible. (For the mitigations, this should be a high-level, policy based analysis).
https://orca.security/resources/blog/aws-glue-vulnerability/

A feature in AWS Glue can be used to obtain credentials for roles in one's own AWS account to gain full access to internal service APIs. Combined with an internal misconfiguration in Glue's internal service API, the ability to escalate privileges within an account to unrestricted access to all resources of a service in the region, as well as full administrative privileges.

As for mitigation measures，It is necessary to formulate targeted technical and management standards in combination with the characteristics of cloud computing services to reduce hidden dangers. Consolidate the authority control, configuration management, data access, cloud service monitoring, audit notification mechanism of the cloud computing platform. The construction of the cryptographic support system in cloud computing services, the business continuity requirements of cloud platforms and service level agreements, etc., all need to conduct more in-depth research and gradually form a standard system to ensure the security of cloud computing services.
